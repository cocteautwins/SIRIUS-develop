\subsection{Detailed Description}
Representation of the local operator. 

The following functionality is implementated\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item application of the local part of Hamiltonian (kinetic + potential) to the wave-\/fucntions in the P\+P-\/\+P\+W case
\item application of the interstitial part of H and O in the case of F\+P-\/\+L\+A\+P\+W
\item remapping of potential and unit-\/step functions from fine to coarse mesh of G-\/vectors 
\end{DoxyItemize}

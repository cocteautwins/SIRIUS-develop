Detailed Description

Stores <G+k | beta> expansion.

Todo:
Beta_projectors and Beta_projectors_gradient need some rethinking. Beta_projectors are used in two places: in application of non-local potential and in generation of density matrix. Beta_projectors_gradient are used in the calculation of forces. Both are split in chunks, both require an inner product with wave-functions.
